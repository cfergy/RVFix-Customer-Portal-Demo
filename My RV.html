Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset the circuit breaker?

Locate the main breaker panel and toggle the affected breaker off and then on again.

What should I do if my slide-out is stuck?

Ensure your battery is fully charged and check for obstructions. Contact support if the issue persists.

How can I sanitize my water system?

Flush the system with a solution of bleach and water, let sit for 12 hours, then flush thoroughly with clean water.
